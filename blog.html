Our Certificate & online Program For 2024

At our cab and bike service, we prioritize the safety, comfort, and satisfaction of our riders. To maintain the highest standards, we have established a certification program for our valued passengers. In this blog post, we will briefly discuss the purpose, process, and benefits of this certification.


Our rider certification aims to ensure that all passengers using our cab and bike services are well-informed about the guidelines, policies, and features of our platform. This will help create a seamless and enjoyable experience for both riders and drivers, ultimately enhancing the overall quality of our services.


The certification process is designed to be simple, quick, and accessible for all riders. Here's a step-by-step breakdown:a. Visit our dedicated certification webpage or mobile app. b. Create or log in to your user account. c. Review the guidelines, policies, and frequently asked questions. d. Complete a short quiz to test your understanding of the information provided. e. Upon successful completion of the quiz, you will receive a digital certification badge.


We encourage all our riders to participate in the certification program to maximize their experience with our cab and bike services. By doing so, you contribute to a more harmonious and efficient ecosystem for everyone involved. Remember, a certified rider is a responsible and satisfied rider!
